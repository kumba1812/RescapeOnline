About RESCAPE

Modern, clean, premium, and student-friendly accommodation discovery.

Executive Summary

RESCAPE is a digital platform designed to transform how students search for and secure private student accommodation in South Africa. The platform bridges students, universities, and private accommodation providers by creating a trusted, transparent, and student-focused accommodation marketplace.

Problem

Thousands of students struggle to find suitable private accommodation, relying on outdated lists and word-of-mouth. RESCAPE reduces uncertainty, financial risk, and poor living conditions by providing verified listings, images, distances, reviews and transport information.

Value Proposition

For Students
  • Free and easy access to verified accommodation
  • Reduced risk of scams
  • Better decision-making via reviews and transparency
