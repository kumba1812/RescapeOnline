## RescapeOnline

 RESCAPE – Executive Summary
Overview
RESCAPE is a digital platform designed to transform how students search for and secure private student
accommodation in South Africa. The platform bridges the gap between students, universities, and private
accommodation providers by creating a trusted, transparent, and student-focused accommodation marketplace.
For many students, finding accommodation is stressful, expensive, and often unreliable due to limited information,
lack of physical access to properties, and outdated university accommodation lists. RESCAPE solves this problem by
giving students a modern, centralized platform where they can confidently explore, compare, and secure
accommodation options from anywhere in the world.
Problem Statement
Thousands of students across South Africa struggle every year to find suitable private student accommodation. Most
students are forced to rely on basic PDF lists or word-of-mouth recommendations without knowing:
• What the accommodation actually looks like
• How safe the area is
• The distance from campus
• Access to transport and essential amenities
• The quality of student living experiences at the property
This creates uncertainty, wasted time, financial risk, and poor living conditions for many students — especially those
relocating from different provinces or countries who cannot physically view accommodation before arriving.
At the same time, universities face challenges managing accommodation referrals, while landlords struggle to market
their properties directly to students in a credible and trusted environment.
The RESCAPE Solution
RESCAPE provides a centralized website and mobile application where students can discover verified private student
accommodation with complete transparency and convenience.
The platform will allow students to:
• View high-quality images and virtual tours of accommodation
• Compare accommodation options easily
• See the exact distance from campus
• Locate nearby amenities such as grocery stores, gyms, transport routes, and medical facilities
• Access shuttle and transport information
• Read ratings and reviews from previous student tenants
• Make informed housing decisions before relocating
Unlike traditional accommodation listings, RESCAPE focuses specifically on the student experience, ensuring that the
platform highlights the factors students care about most.
Value Proposition
For Students
• Free and easy access to verified accommodation
• Reduced risk of scams and poor living conditions
• Better decision-making through reviews and transparency
• Convenient online access from anywhere in the country or internationally
